Music

Bright Nights and City Lights and You're Talking About are two songs in the Lo-fi genre I wrote and produced during my freshman year of college. I used a free DAW, "LMMS" to produce them.

The Elevated Sessions are 4 jam tracks I produced in succession over the course of 4 days near the end of my Junior year in college inspired by sunny weather and the change from spring to summer. Composed, recorded, and played by me, produced and mixed on Logic Pro X.

My final project for Studio III at the University of Vermont was to record and produce a song of our choice, on Pro Tools. I performed a cover of Stolen Dance by Milky Chance, recorded and mixed by me on Avid Pro Tools.

I made an atmospheric psychadellic-esque song to represent myself in the cold Vermont winters. Palm Tree in the Snow features my vocals, composition, recording, and creativity. Mixed and Produced on Logic Pro X.
